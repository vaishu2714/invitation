About International School of Technology and Sciences for Women
International School of Technology and Sciences (ISTS) for Women is a private engineering college
located at Rajahmundry in East Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh. The college is affiliated to JNTU,
Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The college offers undergraduate courses in
engineering in three branches. International School of Technology & Sciences (ISTS) offers a global
approach to education and research, with a focus on international perspectives and expertise



International School of Technology and Sciences Departments

ISTS has the following academic departments:
Computer Science & Engineering
Electronics & Communication Engineering
Electrical & Electronics Engineering
Humanities & Sciences Engineering
